Day Theme

The theme of the day depends on the context, as it can refer to a specific productivity method , an individual day's theme, or a national holiday. Today's themes include National Chicken Fried Steak Day, National Financial Crime Fighter Day, National Microneedling Day, National Mule Day, and National Pumpkin Day. In a more abstract sense, a daily theme can be related to a productivity method called "day theming" which involves batching similar tasks together, or a philosophical theme centered on focusing on the present day.

Night theme

A night theme, also known as dark mode or dark theme, is a display setting that uses a dark background with light-colored text, icons, and user interface elements. This setting is designed to reduce eye strain, improve readability in low-light conditions, and save battery power on devices with OLED screens. It is a user preference available on many operating systems, apps, and websites.
